Chicken tenders

Description:

This is a short but sweet recipe on how to make chicken tenders that you can make using a air frytier. The tenders will be crispy on outside but juicy and tender on the inside eat with your choice of sauce or no sauce at all. This recipe will only take 40 minutes including the 30 minutes of cook time. And you get a total of 6 servings that you can eat all for yourself or share with friends and family.

Ingredients:

  • 3½ ounces barbecue-flavored pork rinds
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon barbecue seasoning
  • 1 egg, beater
  • 1½ pounds chicken brest tenderloing
  • cooking spray
