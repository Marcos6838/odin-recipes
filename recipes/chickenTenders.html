Chicken Tenders

Description:

There are plenty of reasons to make baked chicken tenders instead of breaking out your deep fryer: They require a lot less oil than their deep-fried counterparts, which makes them a lighter alternative. Plus, this oven-baked method is incredibly easy (not to mention it makes cleanup a breeze).

Ingredients:

  • cooking spray
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 1/4 cups panko bread crumbs
  • 2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground paprika
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 4 skinless,boneless chicken tenders, cut into 1/2-inch strips lengthwise

Steps

  1. Make Breading: Crack a whole egg into a shallo dish. In a zip-top bac, combine the bread crumbs, garlic powder, ionion powder, paprika, slat, and pepper. Mix well.
  2. Dredge Chicken: Dip the chicken in the egg (on each side), place in the zip-top bag, seal, and shake to coat. Place the coated tenders on a foil-lined baking sheet. Repeat with the remaining chicken tenderloins. Spray each tender with cooking spray.
  3. Backe Chicken Tenders: Bake the chicken tenders at 450 degrees F for about 7 minutes on each side. Broil the chicken for about 2 more minutes for an extra crunch.
  4. How long to bake chicken tenders: This recipe will produce perfectly baked chicken tenders in under 20 minutes in an oven preheated to 450 degrees F.
