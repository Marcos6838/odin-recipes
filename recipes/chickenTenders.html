Chicken tenders

Description:

This is a short but sweet recipe on how to make chicken tenders that you can make using a air frytier. The tenders will be crispy on outside but juicy and tender on the inside eat with your choice of sauce or no sauce at all. This recipe will only take 40 minutes including the 30 minutes of cook time. And you get a total of 6 servings that you can eat all for yourself or share with friends and family.

Ingredients:

  • 3½ ounces barbecue-flavored pork rinds
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon barbecue seasoning
  • 1 egg, beater
  • 1½ pounds chicken brest tenderloing
  • cooking spray

Instructions:

  1. Preheat an air fryter to 370 degrees F (190 degrees C).

  2. Place pork rinds in a food processor and pulse to the size of bread crumbs. Place in a shallow bowl. Mix flour and barbecue seasoning together in a shallow dish. Beat egg in a third shallow bowl.

  3. Coat each chicken tendedr first in the flour mixture, then dip in beaten egg, and finally coat in pork rind crumbs.

  4. Spray the basket of the air fryer with nonstick cooking spray. Arrange 1/2 of the chicken tenders in the air fryer basket, making sure none are touching. Spray the top of each tender with cooking spray. Cook until chicken is cooked through and no longer pink at the center, about 15 minutes. Repeat with remaining tenders.
